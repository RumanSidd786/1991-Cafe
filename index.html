Our Story

A warm corner of Lucknow where flavours linger.

1991 Cafe & Lounge was built around a simple idea: great food deserves great company. Tucked away on Sitapur Road, our brick walls, plush leather seating and golden lantern glow set the stage for unhurried meals with friends and family.

From smoky tandoor classics to wok-fired Chinese, fresh-baked pizzas and creamy pastas — every plate is cooked to order with ingredients we'd happily serve our own families.
